FAQ

Common questions

How does Meetory capture my meetings?
▾
Meetory connects to your calendar and integrates with tools like Google Meet, Zoom, and Teams. You can also invite the Meetory bot to any call. Audio is processed securely and transcripts are stored only in your workspace. Nothing is shared externally.
Do I need to change how I run my meetings?
▾
No. Meetory works passively in the background. Run your meetings exactly as you do today. The AI extracts structure after the call — you just review and act on what it surfaces.
How accurate are the AI summaries and task extractions?
▾
Very high accuracy for clear spoken commitments and decisions. For ambiguous items, Meetory surfaces them for human review rather than auto-creating tasks. You stay in control — the AI reduces noise, you make the calls.
Who can see my meeting data?
▾
Your data lives in your workspace and is accessible only to members you invite. Meetory does not use your meeting content to train AI models. Enterprise plans offer SSO, audit logs, and custom data residency.
Does Meetory integrate with our existing tools?
▾
Yes. Meetory integrates with Linear, Jira, Notion, Slack, Google Calendar, Outlook, Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. API access is available on Pro and above for custom integrations.
Is there a free plan?
▾
Yes. The Starter plan is free and includes up to 20 hours of meeting processing per month, full task tracking, and search. Paid plans unlock unlimited meetings, team features, integrations, and advanced AI capabilities.
