FAQ

Common questions.

Meetory is an AI meeting workspace. It joins your calls, transcribes them, and automatically extracts summaries, decisions, and action items — then routes tasks to Linear, Jira, or Notion. Think of it as the layer between your calendar and your task tracker.
Meetory listens for commitments, assignments, and follow-ups during the conversation. Each extracted item is scored by confidence. Items above your threshold are auto-created; others land in an "AI findings to confirm" queue where you review before they become tasks.
